Multicloud
Features Services


Welcome to the Multicloud app. This app allows you to access cloud storage services, and transfer files between them, or to and from your device.

Mutlicloud lets you manage files between multiple cloud storae services, such as DropBox, Google Drive, OneDrive, and Box.


To begin, click on the Sign Up button to create an account. If you already have an account, click on the Sign In button.


To associate services with your account, click on the menu icon, and navigate to the Manage Cloud Storage page.

Enter your cloud account details, and then navigate to the File Transfer page to begin managing files.
