Welcome to the Multicloud App

With Multicloud, you can manage files between multiple cloud storage services, such as DropBox, Google Drive, OneDrive, and Box


To begin, click on the Sign up button to create an account. If you already have an account, click on Sign In button


To associate services with your account, click on the menu icon in the top bar, and navigate to the Manage Cloud Storage Page. Enter your Cloud account details. You must be signed into Multicloud app to do this.


To manage or transfer files, click on the menu icon in the top bar, and navigate to the File Transfer Page. You must be signed into Multicloud app, and have at least one Cloud account configured & added.
